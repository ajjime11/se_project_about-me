Travel

From a young age, the world has been my playground. I've traversed continents, immersing myself in the vibrant tapestry of diverse cultures. From savoring authentic street food in Mexico to marveling at ancient ruins in Italy and witnessing the passion of tango in Argentina, each journey has been a transformative experience. Whether it's through sampling local cuisine, engaging in traditional celebrations, or simply observing daily life, I've gained a profound appreciation for the richness and variety of human experience. My wanderlust is insatiable, and I'm eager to continue exploring the world, collecting stories and memories along the way.

Sports

Sports have been a cornerstone of my life since I was a young child. My journey began with basketball, dribbling and shooting hoops in a YMCA league at the tender age of four. I also embraced other sports, spending summers honing my skills at swim & dive camps and creating lasting memories on the little league baseball field. Though soccer ultimately captured my heart, leading me to become a devoted Chelsea fan, the lessons learned from various sports have shaped me profoundly. Beyond the thrill of competition, sports instilled in me the values of teamwork, discipline, and resilience, fostering camaraderie and a lifelong passion for athletic pursuits.

Video Games

Video games have been a lifelong companion, sparking joy and camaraderie since my early days with Crash Bandicoot on the PlayStation 1. From countless Mario Party battles to intense Call of Duty: Zombies sessions and epic Super Smash Bros showdowns, gaming has always been a social glue, bonding me with friends and creating unforgettable memories.My passion for gaming even led me to competitive success, reaching the top 1% in both League of Legends and Overwatch, and proudly representing my university's team in a national tournament. Though I've shifted away from the competitive scene, gaming remains an integral part of my life, offering a fun and relaxing escape.

Music

Music has been a constant companion, weaving a soundtrack to my life's journey. From my first iPod nano to the cherished "Meteora" album gifted by my grandma, each song has held a special meaning. Inspired by my brother's love for bands like Sum 41, Nirvana, and Blink-182, I delved into the world of rock music, attending unforgettable concerts and immersing myself in the energy and emotion of live performances. While rock remains a cornerstone of my musical taste, my love for music extends beyond genre boundaries. I find myself equally captivated by the pulsating beats of EDM artists like Martin Garrix, DJ Snake, and David Guetta, and the raw lyricism of Hip-Hop/Rap icons like Travis Scott, Drake, and Gunna.
